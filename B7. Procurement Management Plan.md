## 3. MAKE-OR-BUY ANALYSIS

### 3.1 Make vs Buy Decisions

|Item|Decision|Justifikasi|
|---|---|---|
|**Software Development**|MAKE|Core competency, IP ownership, customization|
|**Cloud Infrastructure**|BUY|Not core, scalable, cost-effective|
|**Database License**|BUY|Required (SQL Server), support needed|
|**SSL Certificate**|BUY|Standard item, vendor expertise|
|**Training Delivery**|MAKE|Custom content, team expertise|
|**Security Testing**|MAKE + BUY|Internal QA + external pen test|
|**Documentation**|MAKE|Team knowledge required|

**Kriteria**: **MAKE** kalau: Core competency, IP critical, long-term need. **BUY** kalau: Commodity, specialized expertise, one-time need.

### 3.2 Make-or-Buy Decision Matrix

**Faktor yang Dipertimbangkan**: Cost (total cost of ownership), Risk (vendor dependency), Control (IP, customization), Expertise (availability in-house), Time (lead time, availability), Quality (standards, reliability).

**Contoh: Cloud Infrastructure** Cost: Buy lebih murah dari build (no capex). Risk: Medium (vendor lock-in mitigated by multi-cloud ready). Control: Adequate (admin access, APIs). Expertise: Vendor punya specialized expertise. Time: Immediate availability. Quality: High (SLA 99,9% uptime). **Decision**: BUY ✓

## 5. VENDOR SELECTION CRITERIA

### 5.1 Prequalification Criteria

**Mandatory Requirements**:

1. **Legal Entity**: Registered company dengan NPWP
2. **Financial Health**: Financial statements (last 2 years)
3. **Experience**: Minimum 2 tahun di similar products/services
4. **References**: At least 2 client references
5. **Compliance**: No blacklist di LPSE (government e-procurement)

**Untuk Technical Vendors (Software, Infrastructure)**: 6. **Certifications**: ISO 27001 (security), ISO 9001 (quality) preferred 7. **Technical Expertise**: Certified engineers/consultants 8. **Support**: 24/7 support availability (untuk critical items)

### 5.2 Evaluation Criteria (Weighted Scoring)

|Criteria|Weight|Description|
|---|---|---|
|**Price**|40%|Competitive pricing, value for money|
|**Technical Capability**|25%|Product quality, features, performance|
|**Experience**|15%|Track record, similar projects, client references|
|**Delivery**|10%|Lead time, availability, responsiveness|
|**Support**|10%|Warranty, maintenance, SLA, training|
|**TOTAL**|**100%**|Minimum score to qualify: 70/100|

**Scoring**: 1-5 scale per criteria - 5 = Excellent, 4 = Good, 3 = Adequate, 2 = Poor, 1 = Unacceptable.

### 5.3 Selection Process

**Step 1: RFI/RFQ Issuance** - Request for Information (untuk complex items), Request for Quotation (untuk standard items). Deadline: 7-14 hari untuk response.

**Step 2: Proposal Evaluation** - Technical team review, scoring against criteria, shortlist top 3 vendors.

**Step 3: Negotiation** - Price negotiation, terms & conditions, SLA discussion.

**Step 4: Award** - CCB approval (kalau >Rp 10 juta), PPK approval, contract signing.

## 6. CONTRACT TYPES

### 6.1 Contract Type Selection

**Fixed-Price (FP)**: Use untuk: Well-defined scope, standard items. Examples: SSL Certificate, Software Licenses, Printing. Risk: Vendor bears cost risk. Advantage: Budget certainty.

**Time & Materials (T&M)**: Use untuk: Uncertain scope, ongoing services. Examples: Cloud hosting, Internet, Consulting. Risk: Buyer bears cost risk. Advantage: Flexibility. Control: Monthly cap, approval untuk overruns.

**Cost-Reimbursable** (not used di project ini): Too risky untuk government projects.

## 8. CONTRACT ADMINISTRATION

### 8.1 Contract Management Responsibilities

**Project Manager**: Overall contract oversight, vendor relationship management, performance monitoring, payment approval.

**Procurement Lead** (Asst. PM): Contract documentation, vendor communication, purchase orders, invoice processing.

**Technical Lead** (SA): Technical acceptance, quality verification, issue escalation.

**Finance** (BSN Finance Team): Payment processing, budget tracking, audit compliance.

### 8.2 Contract Performance Monitoring

**Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)**:

**Untuk Cloud Infrastructure** (PROC-01,02,07): Uptime: >99,5%. Response Time: <2 detik avg. Support Response: <4 jam untuk P1 issues. Measurement: Monthly monitoring reports.

**Untuk Software Licenses** (PROC-03,08): Delivery: On-time (within 7 hari). Activation: Successful (no issues). Support: Responsive (24-hour email response).

**Untuk Professional Services** (PROC-06,09,15): Quality: Deliverable acceptance rate >90%. Timeline: On schedule. Expertise: Certified professionals assigned.

**Untuk Operational Items** (PROC-10-14): Quality: As specified (no defects). Delivery: On time dan complete. Price: Within budget.

### 8.3 Performance Reviews

## 9. VENDOR RELATIONSHIP MANAGEMENT

### 9.1 Communication Protocol

**Regular Meetings**: **Critical Vendors** (Cloud, SQL Server): Bi-weekly sync. **Standard Vendors**: Monthly check-in. **Ad-hoc**: As needed untuk issues.

**Escalation Path**:

1. **Level 1**: PM ↔ Vendor Account Manager
2. **Level 2**: PPK ↔ Vendor Director
3. **Level 3**: BSN Direktur ↔ Vendor C-Suite

### 9.2 Issue Management

**Issue Categories**: **P1 Critical**: Service down, project blocker → 4-hour response. **P2 High**: Major function impaired → 1-day response. **P3 Medium**: Minor issue → 3-day response. **P4 Low**: Enhancement request → 1-week response.

**Issue Log**: Tracked di JIRA, reported weekly.

## 10. LEGAL & REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

### 10.1 Government Procurement Regulations

**Perpres 12/2021** - Pengadaan Barang/Jasa Pemerintah: **Principles**: Efficient, effective, transparent, open, competitive, fair, accountable. **Methods**: E-procurement via LPSE (>Rp 50 juta), Direct procurement (<Rp 50 juta, standard items), Direct appointment (sole source, justified).

**Compliance Requirements**: Semua vendors verified di LPSE system. No blacklisted vendors. Fair competition (min 3 bidders preferred). Transparent evaluation. Documented justification. PPK approval mandatory.

### 10.2 BSN Internal Policies

**Approval Thresholds**: <Rp 10 juta: PM approval; Rp 10-50 juta: PPK approval; >Rp 50 juta: Direktur approval + LPSE tender.

**Documentation**: Procurement request form, vendor evaluation matrix, contract signed by authorized signatory, acceptance certificate (BAST), invoice & payment proof.

### 10.3 Contract Terms (Standard Clauses)

**Payment Terms**: Net 30 hari from invoice + acceptance. Withholding tax per Indonesian tax law. Payment via bank transfer (no cash).

**Warranty**: Hardware: Minimum 1 tahun. Software: Support for subscription period. Services: 90-day defect warranty.

**Liability**: Vendor liability untuk service failures. Force majeure clause. Dispute resolution (arbitration).

**Termination**: Termination for cause (breach). Termination for convenience (30-day notice). Final settlement procedures.

**Intellectual Property**: Project deliverables owned by BSN. Vendor tools/IP remain vendor's. License grants clearly defined.

## 11. RISK MANAGEMENT UNTUK PROCUREMENT

### 11.1 Procurement Risks

|Risk|Probabilitas|Impact|Mitigation|
|---|---|---|---|
|Vendor delay|Sedang|Tinggi|Backup vendors identified, early ordering|
|Price increase|Rendah|Sedang|Fixed-price contracts, lock prices early|
|Vendor bankruptcy|Rendah|Tinggi|Financial health check, diversify vendors|
|Quality issues|Sedang|Sedang|Acceptance testing, warranty clauses|
|Vendor lock-in|Sedang|Sedang|Open standards, multi-vendor strategy|

### 11.2 Vendor Contingency Plans

**Critical Vendors** (Cloud, SQL Server): Backup Vendor: Identified dan pre-qualified. Migration Plan: Documented (bisa switch dalam 1 minggu). Data Export: Regular backups, portable formats.

**Standard Vendors**: Multiple options available di market. Switch dengan minimal impact.
