## 1. RINGKASAN EKSEKUTIF

Dokumen ini mengatur bagaimana risks proyek KANMIS 2.2 akan kita identifikasi, analisis, respond, dan monitor untuk meminimalkan ancaman dan memaksimalkan peluang.

**Filosofi Risk kita**: "Proactive risk management lebih murah daripada reactive crisis management."

KAK halaman 9-10 mengidentifikasi critical dependencies (SIMPONI integration, data migration) yang menjadi major project risks.

## 2. PENDEKATAN RISK MANAGEMENT

### 2.1 Strategi Risk Management

**Preventive Focus**: Identify dan mitigate risks sebelum terjadi. **Continuous Process**: Risk management throughout project lifecycle. **Stakeholder Involvement**: Engage team + PPK dalam risk identification. **Data-Driven**: Pakai historical data, expert judgment, quantitative analysis.

### 2.2 Risk Management Processes (PMBOK)

1. **Plan Risk Management** → Dokumen ini
2. **Identify Risks** → Risk register creation
3. **Perform Qualitative Analysis** → Probability & Impact assessment
4. **Perform Quantitative Analysis** → Expected Monetary Value (EMV)
5. **Plan Risk Responses** → Response strategies per risk
6. **Implement Risk Responses** → Execute response plans
7. **Monitor Risks** → Track identified risks, identify new risks

## 4. RISK IDENTIFICATION

### 4.1 Teknik Identifikasi Risk

**Brainstorming Sessions**: Who: PM, SA, SP1-3, DBA, QA. When: Week 1, terus monthly. Duration: 2 jam. Output: Initial risk list.

**Checklists** (dari similar projects): SIMPONI integration projects (Kemenkeu), BSN previous IT projects, Industry best practices (Gartner, PMI).

**SWOT Analysis**: Strengths: Experienced team, clear requirements. Weaknesses: Tight schedule, complex integration. Opportunities: Process improvement, innovation. Threats: SIMPONI changes, resource turnover.

**Expert Interviews**: BSN IT staff (previous system knowledge), Kemenkeu SIMPONI team, External consultants.

**Assumption Analysis**: Challenge project assumptions. What if assumptions salah?

### 4.2 Kategori Risk (dari RBS)

**Technical (40% of risks)**: SIMPONI API changes, QR Digital Signature complexity, Database migration errors, Performance bottlenecks.

**Project Management (30%)**: Schedule compression, Budget constraints, Scope changes, Resource conflicts.

**Organizational (20%)**: Stakeholder approval delays, BSN policy changes, Team turnover.

**External (10%)**: Kemenkeu schedule changes, Regulatory updates, Vendor delays.

## 5. RISK ANALYSIS

### 5.1 Qualitative Risk Analysis

**Probability Scale** (1-5):

1. **Very Low** (0-10% chance)
2. **Low** (10-30%)
3. **Medium** (30-50%)
4. **High** (50-70%)
5. **Very High** (70-100%)

**Impact Scale** (1-5):

1. **Very Low** (negligible impact)
2. **Low** (<5% cost/time impact)
3. **Medium** (5-10% impact)
4. **High** (10-20% impact)
5. **Very High** (>20% impact)

**Probability & Impact (P&I) Matrix**:

```
Impact →
        1    2    3    4    5
P   5   M    H    H    VH   VH
r   4   M    M    H    H    VH
o   3   L    M    M    H    H
b   2   L    L    M    M    H
    1   VL   L    L    M    M

VL = Very Low (score 1-5)
L = Low (6-10)
M = Medium (11-15)
H = High (16-20)
VH = Very High (21-25)
```

### 5.2 Quantitative Risk Analysis

**Expected Monetary Value (EMV)**: EMV = Probability × Impact (dalam Rupiah)

**Contoh**: Risk: SIMPONI integration delay (2 minggu). Probability: 60% (High). Impact: Rp 25 juta (additional resources). EMV = 0,60 × 25 juta = **Rp 15 juta**.

**Contingency Reserve Calculation**: Sum of all EMVs = Total contingency needed.

## 7. RISK RESPONSE STRATEGIES

### 7.1 Response Plans untuk Top Risks

#### R01: SIMPONI Integration (VH Risk)

**Strategy**: MITIGATE + TRANSFER

Mitigate:

- Early engagement dengan Kemenkeu di minggu pertama
- Joint technical workshop di minggu ke-2
- Mockup API testing di minggu ke-3
- Parallel development (mock vs real API)
- Weekly sync meetings dengan SIMPONI team

Transfer:

- SLA agreement dengan Kemenkeu
- Escalation protocol to Direktur level kalau needed

Contingency: +5 hari buffer untuk integration. Budget Reserve: Rp 25 juta.

#### R02: Data Migration (H Risk)

**Strategy**: MITIGATE

Actions:

- Data quality assessment di minggu 1-2
- Mapping document review (SA + DBA)
- Test migration di staging di minggu ke-8
- Automated data validation scripts
- Rollback plan documented
- Incremental migration approach

Contingency: +3 hari buffer. Budget Reserve: Rp 15 juta.

#### R03: Resource Turnover (M Risk)

**Strategy**: MITIGATE + ACCEPT

Mitigate:

- Competitive compensation
- Clear career path
- Recognition program
- Work-life balance
- Knowledge transfer (documentation, pairing)
- Backup resource identified untuk setiap key role

Accept: Have recruitment pipeline ready. Contingency: 2 minggu untuk replacement ramp-up. Budget Reserve: Rp 20 juta.

#### R04: Scope Creep (H Risk)

**Strategy**: AVOID

Actions:

- Clear requirements baseline di minggu 1-2
- Formal change control process (CCB)
- Change request template mandatory
- Impact analysis sebelum approval
- Stakeholder education on scope baseline
- Regular scope validation meetings
- "No" is an acceptable answer

Budget Reserve: Rp 20 juta (strict control).

#### R05: QR Digital Signature (M Risk)

**Strategy**: MITIGATE

Actions:

- Proof-of-concept early di minggu ke-4
- Security expert consultation
- Pakai proven cryptography libraries
- Code review by security tester
- Penetration testing
- Vendor support contract untuk libraries

Contingency: Alternative approach (standard digital signature). Budget Reserve: Rp 10 juta.

### 7.2 General Response Strategies

**AVOID**: Eliminate the threat - Change project plan, add resources, extend timeline.

**MITIGATE**: Reduce probability or impact - Training, prototypes, best practices, quality processes.

**TRANSFER**: Shift risk to third party - Insurance, contracts, outsourcing, SLAs.

**ACCEPT**: Acknowledge, prepare contingency - Active: Reserve funds/time; Passive: Deal with it kalau happens.

**EXPLOIT** (untuk opportunities): Assign best resources, fast-track schedule.

**ENHANCE** (untuk opportunities): Increase probability, identify triggers.

**SHARE** (untuk opportunities): Partnerships, joint ventures.

## 8. RISK MONITORING & CONTROL

### 8.1 Risk Monitoring Procedures

**Weekly Risk Review**: PM review risk register. New risks identified? Risk status changed? Responses effective? Update risk scores.

**Monthly Risk Workshop**: Team brainstorm new risks. Reassess existing risks. Update response plans. Report ke PPK.

**Continuous Monitoring**: Watch untuk risk triggers. Track leading indicators. Early warning system.

### 8.2 Risk Reporting

**Risk Dashboard** (weekly update): Top 10 risks (P&I matrix visual), risk trend (increasing/decreasing), response status, reserve utilization.

**Risk Section di Status Report**: New risks identified, risks materialized, response actions taken, risk closure.

**Risk Alert** (as needed): High/VH risk materialized, immediate notification ke PPK, response plan activation.

### 8.3 Risk Triggers & Leading Indicators

|Risk|Trigger|Leading Indicator|
|---|---|---|
|SIMPONI delay|Kemenkeu email tentang schedule|Slow response ke tech queries|
|Scope creep|Frequent change requests|Ambiguous requirements|
|Resource turnover|Resume updates di LinkedIn|Decreased engagement|
|Budget overrun|CPI < 0,95|Increasing burn rate|
|Schedule delay|SPI < 0,95|Critical path slippage|

## 10. RISK OWNERSHIP & ACCOUNTABILITY

### 10.1 Risk Roles

**Project Manager**: Overall risk management, risk register maintenance, risk reporting, escalation ke PPK.

**System Analyst**: Technical risk identification, technical response planning, integration risk owner.

**Team Members**: Report risks in their domain, implement response actions, monitor assigned risks.

**PPK**: Approve high-risk responses, approve reserve usage (>Rp 10 juta), escalation point.

### 10.2 Risk Owner Assignments

Setiap risk di register punya designated owner yang responsible untuk: Monitor risk triggers, implement response plan, report risk status, escalate kalau needed.

## 11. RISK APPETITE & TOLERANCE

### 11.1 BSN Risk Appetite

**Low Risk Appetite untuk**: Security (zero tolerance), Data integrity (zero tolerance), Regulatory compliance (zero tolerance).

**Moderate Risk Appetite untuk**: Technology choices (proven tech preferred), Schedule (some flexibility acceptable).

**Higher Risk Appetite untuk**: Innovation (QR signature, modern UI), Process improvements.

### 11.2 Risk Thresholds

**Budget Variance**: Hijau: ±5%; Kuning: 5-10%; Merah: >10% (escalate ke PPK).

**Schedule Variance**: Hijau: ±5%; Kuning: 5-10%; Merah: >10% (escalate ke PPK).

**Quality**: Critical defects: 0 (zero tolerance); High defects: <5; Medium defects: <20.
