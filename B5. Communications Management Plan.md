## 4. METODE & TOOLS KOMUNIKASI

### 4.1 Communication Tools

**Email** (primary formal communication): Tool: Microsoft Outlook / Gmail. Purpose: Korespondensi formal, reports, approvals. Response SLA: 24 jam (business days).

**Instant Messaging**: Tool: Slack / Microsoft Teams. Purpose: Quick questions, daily coordination. Channels: #general, #development, #testing, #issues. Response SLA: 2 jam (during work hours).

**Project Management**: Tool: JIRA / Trello. Purpose: Task tracking, sprint planning, defect management. Update frequency: Daily.

**Document Repository**: Tool: Google Drive / SharePoint. Purpose: Document storage, version control. Structure: /Project Charter, /Design Docs, /Reports, /Deliverables.

**Video Conferencing**: Tool: Zoom / Google Meet. Purpose: Remote meetings, presentations, training. Recording: Yes (with consent).

**Dashboard**: Tool: Excel Power BI / Google Data Studio. Purpose: Real-time project metrics. Access: PM, PPK, Direktur BSN. Update: Real-time (automated).

### 4.2 Communication Channels by Type

**Urgent (Merah)**: Method: Phone call + Slack ping + Email. Response: Immediate (<2 jam). Examples: Critical defect, security breach, blocker issue.

**Important (Kuning)**: Method: Email + Slack message. Response: Same day. Examples: Risk materialized, schedule slip, change request.

**Normal (Hijau)**: Method: Email atau scheduled meeting. Response: 1-2 business days. Examples: Status updates, routine questions, documentation.

## 5. JADWAL MEETING

### 5.1 Regular Meetings

#### Daily Standup

Time: 9:00-9:15 AM (Mon-Fri). Duration: 15 menit. Participants: PM, SA, Developers. Agenda: Kemarin ngapain (2 min), Hari ini mau ngapain (2 min), Ada blocker apa (1 min). Output: Updated task status di JIRA.

#### Weekly Status Meeting

Time: Jumat 14:00-16:00. Duration: 2 jam. Participants: Semua project team. Agenda: Week accomplishments (15 min), Metrics review EVM, quality (20 min), Issues & risks (30 min), Next week plan (20 min), Q&A (35 min). Output: Weekly status report.

#### Bi-Weekly Steering Committee

Time: Setiap 2 minggu, Rabu 10:00-11:30. Duration: 1,5 jam. Participants: PM, PPK, Direktur BSN (optional), SA. Agenda: Progress update (20 min), Budget status (15 min), Risk & issues (20 min), Decisions needed (20 min), Next period focus (15 min). Output: Meeting minutes, action items.

#### Monthly Executive Review

Time: Jumat terakhir tiap bulan, 15:00-17:00. Duration: 2 jam. Participants: PM, PPK, Direktur BSN, SA. Agenda: Monthly achievements (20 min), Milestone progress (20 min), Financial status (20 min), Strategic issues (30 min), Next month outlook (20 min), Executive decisions (10 min). Output: Executive summary report.

#### Integration Sync (dengan SIMPONI)

Time: Bi-weekly, Selasa 14:00-15:00. Duration: 1 jam. Participants: SA, SP1, Kemenkeu SIMPONI Tech Lead. Agenda: API specification review, Testing progress, Integration issues, Next sprint planning. Output: Integration sync notes.

### 5.2 Ad-Hoc Meetings

**Change Control Board (CCB)**: Frequency: As needed (waktu ada CR submitted). Duration: 1 jam. Participants: PM, SA, PPK.

**Risk Review**: Frequency: Waktu high-risk identified. Duration: 30-60 min. Participants: PM, SA, relevant team members.

**UAT Workshops**: Frequency: Selama UAT phase (Bulan 5). Duration: Half-day sessions. Participants: PM, SA, QA, 40 LPK representatives.

## 6. KEBUTUHAN REPORTING

### 6.1 Status Reports

#### Daily Status (Internal)

Format: JIRA dashboard update. Content: Task status, hours logged, blockers. Audience: PM, SA. Delivery: EOD (End of Day).

#### Weekly Status Report

Format: Email + PDF attachment. Sections: Executive Summary, Accomplishments minggu ini, Planned untuk next week, EVM metrics (SPI, CPI), Issues & risks (top 5), Milestones status, Photos/screenshots (kalau ada). Audience: PPK, Direktur BSN, Project Team. Delivery: Jumat 17:00. Page Limit: 3-4 halaman.

#### Monthly Executive Summary

Format: PowerPoint presentation. Sections: Project health (RAG status), Key achievements, Milestone progress, Budget vs actual, Schedule vs baseline, Quality metrics, Risks & mitigation, Next month focus, Decisions needed. Audience: Direktur BSN, PPK. Delivery: Jumat terakhir tiap bulan. Slides: 10-12 slides.

#### Milestone Report

Format: Formal document + presentation. Triggered: Di setiap milestone (M1-M5). Sections: Milestone description, Acceptance criteria met, Deliverables submitted, Lessons learned, Next milestone preview. Audience: PPK, Direktur BSN. Delivery: Tanggal milestone achievement.

### 6.2 Financial Reports

#### Budget vs Actual

Format: Excel + dashboard. Frequency: Monthly. Content: CBS breakdown, EVM metrics, forecast. Audience: PM, PPK. Delivery: Tanggal 5 tiap bulan.

#### Invoice & Payment Tracking

Format: Spreadsheet. Frequency: Per termin. Content: Invoice details, payment status, outstanding. Audience: PM, Finance, PPK.

### 6.3 Quality Reports

#### Test Summary Report

Format: PDF document. Frequency: Weekly selama testing phase. Content: Test cases executed, pass/fail rate, defects. Audience: PM, QA, SA.

#### Defect Report

Format: JIRA export + dashboard. Frequency: Daily selama testing. Content: Open defects by severity, aging, trends. Audience: PM, Developers, QA.

## 8. STRATEGI STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT

### 8.1 Engagement Levels

**Manage Closely** (High Power, High Interest): PPK (Habib Nurhasan), Direktur BSN (A. Praba Drijarkara). Strategy: Weekly updates, involve dalam major decisions.

**Keep Satisfied** (High Power, Low Interest): BSN Legal/Compliance. Strategy: Monthly summaries, notify kalau ada regulatory matters.

**Keep Informed** (Low Power, High Interest): BSN IT Staff, LPK Users. Strategy: Newsletters, UAT participation, training.

**Monitor** (Low Power, Low Interest): General BSN staff. Strategy: FYI emails, intranet updates.

### 8.2 Engagement Activities

**Kickoff Meeting** (Bulan 1, Week 1): Semua stakeholders invited. Project overview, roles, communication plan. Q&A session.

**Design Review** (Bulan 1, Week 4): PPK, Direktur, BSN IT. UI/UX mockups presentation. Feedback collection.

**Progress Demos** (Monthly): PPK, Direktur. Live demo completed features. Stakeholder validation.

**UAT Workshops** (Bulan 5): 40 LPK representatives (sample dari 2.700). 3 batches @ 40 peserta. Hands-on testing, feedback.

**Go-Live Briefing** (Bulan 6, pre go-live): Semua stakeholders. Go-live plan, support procedures. Communication strategy.

## 9. CRISIS COMMUNICATION PLAN

### 9.1 Definisi Crisis

**Project Crisis**: Critical defect di production, data breach / security incident, project failure risk (budget >20% overrun, schedule >1 bulan delay), stakeholder escalation, team member accident/emergency.

### 9.2 Crisis Communication Protocol

**Level 1 (Minor)**: PM handle, inform PPK **Level 2 (Moderate)**: PM + PPK handle, inform Direktur **Level 3 (Major)**: Direktur involved, possible BSN-wide communication

**Crisis Communication Steps**:

1. **Assess**: PM evaluasi severity (dalam 1 jam)
2. **Notify**: Inform appropriate level stakeholders (dalam 2 jam)
3. **Plan**: Crisis response meeting (dalam 4 jam)
4. **Communicate**: Stakeholder notification (dalam 8 jam)
5. **Execute**: Implement response plan
6. **Update**: Hourly/daily updates sampai resolved
7. **Debrief**: Post-crisis review, lessons learned

## 11. LESSONS LEARNED & KNOWLEDGE SHARING

### 11.1 Lessons Learned Process

**Collection**: Setelah setiap phase, setelah critical events (issues, risks materialized), end of project.

**Format**: Lessons learned template - Apa yang happened? Kenapa happened? Apa yang should do differently? Siapa yang should know?

**Sharing**: Internal team wiki, BSN knowledge base, Industry conferences (dengan BSN approval).

### 11.2 Knowledge Transfer

**Ke BSN IT Staff** (Bulan 5-6): Technical documentation review, system architecture walkthrough, admin training, support procedures.

**Method**: Workshop sessions (3× full-day), documentation handover, shadowing (BSN IT dengan project team).

## 12. KESIMPULAN & REKOMENDASI

### 12.1 Communication Success Factors

1. **Transparency**: Honest reporting (good & bad news)
2. **Timeliness**: Right information di right time
3. **Clarity**: Avoid jargon, pakai plain language
4. **Consistency**: Regular schedule, standard formats

### 12.2 Rekomendasi

1. Setup communication tools di minggu pertama
2. Conduct stakeholder analysis workshop di minggu pertama
3. Establish communication rhythm (daily/weekly/monthly)
4. Ukur effectiveness, iterate improvements
